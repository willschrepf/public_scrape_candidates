  1. ↑ Note: Michigan Republicans will have a two-step presidential nominating process in 2024. Candidates will run in the state-administered presidential preference primary, but this contest will only determine 16 delegates. The state party's remaining 39 delegates will be allocated based on the results of Republican Party-administered caucuses in each congressional district.
